About me

I've worked in the software engineering field since the beginning of my career, having the opportunity to cover different roles, in development, in support functions, and in management. Such experiences provide me with invaluable advantage in my current activity as Agile Coach.

I got involved in Process & Quality Management field by supporting a major software organisation in the qualification at SW-CMM level 5, and then by leading their transition to CMMI level 5. I've joined more than 20 SCAMPI A, B and C appraisals as Team Member, as well as driving process improvement initiatives.

I've become interested in Agile Mindset since 2007 and, since 2012, I'm helping organisations in the transition to Agile. I achieve this by Training, Mentoring and Coaching, at different organization levels.

During my Agile activities, I've coached more than 20 Scrum/Kanban teams and trained more than 400 people.

I'm a Lifelong learner and never get tired to explore new fields of knowledge.

Location

I'm based in Torino (Italy)
